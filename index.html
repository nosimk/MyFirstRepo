Welcome to Kijabe Hospital!

About Us

We are the first private hospital in Kijabe. We also provide affordable health care services.On top of that, we also have a Lipa Mdogo programme which helps many Kenyans attain proper healthcare without having to take any loans or holding any fundraiser because one can pay their medical bill in installments.

Our Mission

Our Mission is to improve Kenya's healthcare by providing proper and affordable healthcare to many Kenyans.

Our Vision

Our vision is to provide proper health care for all our patients and to save as many lives as possible with affordable healthcare services.
